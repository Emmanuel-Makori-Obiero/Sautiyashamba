🌱 The Problem

Mama Njeri Didn't Fail.
The System Failed Her.

Mama Njeri is sixty-two years old, and her hands are the only insurance policy her family has. In the misty highlands of Karatina, her small plot of kale is more than food — it is her grandson's school fees and her own retirement.

Three weeks ago, a strange yellow fungus began to breathe across her leaves. Her son sent her a YouTube link. But the screen stayed black — the data was off. The link was a door she didn't have the key to.

Out of desperation, she walked to the village center and spent her last savings on a bottle of pesticide she couldn't read, sold by someone who didn't care. The chemical was toxic. By morning, her entire harvest was scorched — blackened.

💡 Mama Njeri's story is the statistical norm for 4.5 million Kenyan farmers. Sauti ya Shamba is the system that should have been there — in her language, on her phone, at zero cost.
⚙️ The Solution

How Sauti ya Shamba Works

Reverse Innovation: cutting-edge AI powering legacy feature phones. No internet. No smartphone. No cost to the farmer.

01📞

Zero-Data Access

Toll-Free IVR or USSD on any basic phone. No data bundles. Works on a KES 500 kabambe.

02🎤

Speak Your Language

Understands Gĩkũyũ, Ekegusii, Swahili — including colloquial names like "mwizi wa fedha" (Spider Mites).

03🧠

KALRO/FAO Verified AI

Answers only from KALRO & FAO-verified manuals. Banned chemicals blocked before spoken.

04🔊

Grandmother-Friendly Voice

Voice cloned from a real local agronomist — warm, slow, clear. 89% of test farmers rated it trustworthy.

05🔁

Human Escalation

Dangerous queries flag a live agronomist. If AI fails, a human callback fires automatically.

06📊

Outbreak Intelligence

Every call feeds a real-time pest-outbreak map sold to governments — funding the free service.
